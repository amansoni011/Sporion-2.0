About Sporion'26

Welcome to Sporion'26

Sporion'26 is the annual sports festival of Engineering College Jhalawar, held from 9 March 2026 to 12 March 2026. The event brings together students to celebrate sportsmanship, teamwork, and healthy competition through games like cricket, basketball, volleyball, sprint, chess, carrom, badminton, tug of war, and marathon.

Teams compete passionately for the prestigious title of “Hunter”, showcasing determination and teamwork. Sporion is not just about winning; it is about the experience, learning, and memories gained through sports. It reminds us that sports teach discipline, respect for time, unity, and togetherness.

Our campus

Our History

Founded in 2022, Sporion was started with the aim of promoting sports and teamwork among students. Over time, it has become one of the most exciting and popular events in the college.

Our Mission

To encourage students to participate in sports and develop discipline, teamwork, and unity through healthy competition.

Our Vision

To make Sporion a platform that inspires sportsmanship, friendship, and a strong sporting spirit among students.
